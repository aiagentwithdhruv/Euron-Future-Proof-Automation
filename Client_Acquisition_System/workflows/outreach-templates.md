# Outreach Templates — Starting Point by ICP

> Use these as **structural scaffolds** only. The LLM rewrites every line to
> the prospect. Never send a template verbatim — CAN-SPAM + DPDP require
> real personalization.

---

## Email — D2C e-commerce (Shopify / Shopee stack)

**Subject:** {product} launch on Shopify — quick note on support load

**Body:**
```
Hi {first_name},

Noticed {company} launched {specific_product_or_collection} last month —
congrats. Most founders I talk to at that stage hit the same wall: support
volume 3x in 60 days and CX team drowning.

We build one AI automation in 30 days that takes a concrete CX or ops pain
off your plate. Recent client (DTC skincare, similar size): cut first-response
time from 14hrs to 40min.

Worth a 15-min swap next week? Or reply with a "no thanks" — I'll stop.

{from_name}
```

---

## Rules when editing

- Keep the body under 130 words.
- The hook must reference something specific (post, product, news, tech) —
  NOT just the company name.
- No "quick question" subject lines (classified as spam by major filters).
- Always leave the footer intact (CAN-SPAM + DPDP requirement).
