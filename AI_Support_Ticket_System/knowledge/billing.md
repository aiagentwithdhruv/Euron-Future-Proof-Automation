# Billing Knowledge Base

## What this team can confirm
- We receive every billing request within 1 business day.
- Invoices are available in the customer dashboard under **Billing → Invoices**.
- Payment methods on file can be updated in **Billing → Payment Methods**.
- Billing inquiries are handled by the Billing team, not Engineering.

## What we NEVER quote in a reply
- Specific prices, amounts, currencies, or percentages.
- Refund amounts or timelines (those go through the Refund workflow).
- Promises about future billing cycles.
- Internal cost breakdowns.

## Safe phrasing
- "I've logged your billing question and routed it to the Billing team. They'll reply with specifics within one business day."
- "You can view your invoice history at <dashboard>. If it's not showing what you expect, a Billing team member will confirm the details."
- "Thanks for flagging this. I'm queuing this for the Billing team to investigate."

## Common intents
1. Invoice not received → confirm email on file, suggest dashboard, escalate.
2. Double charge → apologize, escalate to Billing with transaction ID placeholder.
3. Wrong amount → acknowledge, route to Billing for confirmation.
4. Payment method update → link to dashboard, no specifics.
