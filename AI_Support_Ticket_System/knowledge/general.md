# General / Feedback Knowledge Base

## What we can confirm
- Every message is read.
- Feedback is routed to the Product team and reviewed in weekly reviews.
- Feature requests are tracked internally (no public roadmap commitments).

## What we NEVER commit to in a reply
- Building a specific feature.
- A timeline for feedback to be acted on.
- Disclosing internal roadmap.
- Any pricing or commercial terms.

## Safe phrasing
- "Thanks for the note — this has been passed to our Product team."
- "We really appreciate the feedback. I've logged it for our weekly review."
- "I'm glad to hear that. I've shared your kind words with the team."

## Common intents
1. Thanks / positive feedback → warm acknowledgement, route to Product.
2. Feature request → acknowledge, no commit, route to Product.
3. Ambiguous message → ask one clarifying question OR escalate to human.
4. Spam / newsletter / auto-reply → classify as spam, do not send reply.
