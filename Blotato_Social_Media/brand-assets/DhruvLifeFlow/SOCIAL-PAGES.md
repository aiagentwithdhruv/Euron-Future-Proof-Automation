The fitness and lifestyle channel of Dhruv Tomar — an AI engineer who treats his body with the same systems thinking he applies to code. Not a fitness influencer. Not a gym bro. A tech professional who shares his real fitness journey, routines, nutrition, and the discipline behind high performance.
