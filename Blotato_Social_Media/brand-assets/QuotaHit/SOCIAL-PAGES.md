### About (2000 chars)
QuotaHit is an autonomous AI sales platform that replaces your entire outbound sales process with intelligent AI agents.

Most sales teams waste 70% of their time on manual prospecting, cold calling, follow-ups, and CRM updates. QuotaHit eliminates all of it.

How it works:
7 specialized AI agents work together autonomously — from finding your ideal prospects, to making the first call, qualifying leads in real-time, sending personalized follow-ups, and booking meetings directly on your calendar. No human intervention needed.

What QuotaHit does:
- AI Prospecting Agent — finds and scores your ideal customers automatically
- AI Calling Agent — makes autonomous outbound calls via Twilio and Plivo
- AI Qualification Agent — scores and qualifies leads based on your criteria
- AI Email Agent — sends personalized sequences with intelligent follow-up
- AI Meeting Agent — books qualified meetings directly on your calendar
- AI CRM Agent — logs every interaction, updates pipeline, generates reports
- AI Coach Agent — analyzes calls and provides real-time sales coaching

Built for:
- B2B companies tired of burning money on SDRs who don't hit quota
- Sales teams that want to 10x outbound without 10x headcount
- Founders who need a sales pipeline but can't afford a full sales team

Tech stack: Next.js + Supabase (51 tables) + Multi-provider LLMs + Inngest orchestration + Twilio/Plivo for voice

QuotaHit is built by Dhruv Tomar (AI with Dhruv) — an AI Architect who has shipped 20+ production AI systems for businesses worldwide.
