## Shipping

We ship within 1–2 business days of order confirmation. Delivery
windows depend on destination — within India, expect 3–5 business
days after dispatch; international orders typically arrive within
7–12 business days.

## Returns

Unworn items can be returned within 14 days of delivery for a full
refund or exchange. Sale items are final. Initiate a return from
your order page.

## Sizing

Our garments run true to size. If you're between sizes, most
customers prefer sizing up for a relaxed fit. Each product page has
a size chart with measurements in inches and centimetres.

## Care

Cold wash inside out. Do not tumble dry. Avoid bleach. Iron on low
if needed.

## Support

Reply to any order email or write to support@example.com. A human
reads every message.
