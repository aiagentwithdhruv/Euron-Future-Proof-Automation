# Refund Policy

## Timeline

We offer a full refund within **14 days of delivery** for unused items in their original packaging. Items returned after day 14 are not eligible for a refund and will be shipped back at the customer's cost.

## How to Request

1. Email `support@example.com` with your order ID.
2. Our team replies within 24 business hours with a return label.
3. Drop the package at any courier pickup within 7 days of receiving the label.

## Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within **5–7 business days** of the item arriving at our warehouse and passing a quick quality check.

## Exclusions

- Final-sale and clearance items
- Items marked "non-returnable" on the product page
- Gift cards

## Damaged or Wrong Item

If an item arrives damaged or is the wrong product, email us within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We cover return shipping and replace or refund at your choice.
