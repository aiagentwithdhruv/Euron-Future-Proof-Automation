# Shipping FAQ

## Domestic (India)

We ship pan-India via **Delhivery**. Standard delivery takes **3–5 business days** to metro cities and 5–7 business days to tier-2/3 locations. Shipping is **free for orders above ₹999**; below that, a flat ₹49 fee applies.

## International

We currently ship to the US, UK, UAE, and Singapore via **DHL Express**. International delivery takes **6–9 business days**. Shipping is calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

## Tracking

You receive a tracking link via email and SMS within 24 hours of dispatch. The link updates in near real-time via the courier's network.

## Address Changes

We can update the delivery address if the order has not yet been picked up by the courier. Reply to the order confirmation email with the new address within **2 hours of placing the order**.

## Missed Delivery

The courier makes up to **3 delivery attempts**. After that, the package is returned to our warehouse and we contact you to schedule a reshipment (reshipment is free on prepaid orders, chargeable on COD).
